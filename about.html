About the hotel

Rakaposhi Mountain View Hotel is a family‑run guest house designed for travellers who want quiet, comfortable stays close to nature. The building is inspired by traditional Hunza architecture, with large windows that frame the surrounding peaks.

We offer a small number of carefully prepared rooms to ensure a peaceful atmosphere. Breakfast is included, and dinners can be arranged featuring local ingredients.

Facilities

  • Ensuite bedrooms with mountain view balconies
  • Dining area with panoramic windows
  • Complimentary WiFi and tea service
  • Guided hikes and day trips on request
